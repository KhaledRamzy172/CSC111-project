About Zed Park

Zed Park is a beautiful urban oasis located in the heart of the city. Spanning over 500 acres, it offers lush greenery, serene lakes, and recreational activities for all ages. Whether you're looking to relax, exercise, or enjoy a picnic, Zed Park has something for everyone. Established in 1985, it has become a beloved spot for locals and tourists alike.

Location

Zed Park is located in Sheikh Zayed City, Giza Governorate, Egypt. It's easily accessible by public transport and has ample parking.
